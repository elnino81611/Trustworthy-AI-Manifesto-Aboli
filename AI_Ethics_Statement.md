Professional Declaration of Responsible AI and MLOps Practice
Preamble:

As an Artificial Intelligence and ML/Ops Engineer, I hereby commit myself to upholding the highest standards of professional conduct and ethical deployment across all phases of the machine learning lifecycle.

Core Ethical Commitments
Ethical Fairness and Non-Discrimination: I will dedicate all efforts to ensure that deployed models remain free from systemic bias and discrimination. I commit to providing impartial responses to all individuals, irrespective of their background, skin color, identity, or nationality.

Data Integrity and User Privacy: I commit to absolutely no misuse of user data or violation of user privacy. I will strictly adhere to all established privacy and ethical principles across all user interactions and data handling processes.

System Security and Integrity (MLSecOps): I will utilize all necessary measures to secure user data and the integrity of the models, actively working to prevent hacking and the unauthorized exploitation or misuse of information.

Conclusion:

I consider these core principles to be my fundamental professional duty and the essential guide for all my technical practice.
