**Data & MLOps Governance includes 3 fundamental sections:**

**1. Data Governance, which has 3 subsections:**
   * The first is Data Policies and Accountability in data.
   * The second is Personal Data Ownership which guarantees quality in data.
   * The third is Standard Audits, meaning periodic conformity of data with regulations and data policy-making.

**2. Special focus on Traceability in data, which includes 3 fundamental sections:**
   * First, guaranteeing data quality and preventing data poisoning and logging all data.
   * Second, complete guarantee of all stages for model training and logging all its stages.
   * And finally, logging all decisions in the output and the decision-making thought process.

**3. Sustainability, whose basis is long-term performance:**
   * First, Operational Sustainability: continuous maintenance and updating.
   * Second, Ethical Sustainability and not violating ethical systems.
   * And finally, Environmental Sustainability, which, as much as possible, prevents damage to the environment and excessive production of carbon monoxide and carbon.
